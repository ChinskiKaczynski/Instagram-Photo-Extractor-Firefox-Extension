Instagram Photo Extractor

Click the button below to extract high-resolution photos from the current Instagram post.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Go to Instagram website:
    • Open your web browser and type in the address bar: www.instagram.com.
    • Log in to your account using your username and password.
  2. Find a specific user:
    • In the search bar at the top of the page, type the username of the user whose post you want to view.
    • Click on the username in the search results to go to their profile.
  3. Open a specific post:
    • On the user's profile, find the post from which you want to extract the photo.
    • Click on that post to open it in full-screen mode.
  4. Use the Instagram Photo Extractor extension:
    • Click on the extension icon in the browser's toolbar.
    • In the opened popup window, click the "Extract Photos" button to extract the photo in full resolution.
